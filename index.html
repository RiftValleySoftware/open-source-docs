    AmbiaMara

    App Store

    Instructions

    Documentation

    Source Code

    		 AmbiaMara is a customized timer for public speakers, or for occasions where you are timing speakers/discussion. It has a HUGE digital display that is easily visible across large rooms; even in bright daylight, and on small device displays. AmbiaMara also has "Podium Mode," where three "traffic lights" are displayed. You can select from a set of pre-recorded sounds, or use songs from your music playlist as alarms. It was designed with accessibility and usability in mind. AmbiaMara has been localized in several languages, and has full support for Voiceover Mode in all languages. It also has full support for gestures and haptics. AmbiaMara is also available as a Mac app.
